Features:

-->Interactive Image Queries: Upload any image and ask questions related to the content of the image. Whether it's identifying objects, describing scenes, 	or understanding relationships between elements, our app can handle it all.

-->Advanced VQA Model: Powered by a state-of-the-art Vision-and-Language Transformer (ViLT) model fine-tuned on the VQAv2 dataset, our system is designed to understand and respond to a wide range of visual and textual inputs.

-->Seamless User Experience: The user-friendly interface ensures a smooth and engaging experience. Easily upload images, type in your questions, and receive instant answers.

-->Real-Time Processing: Get answers quickly with our efficient backend, which leverages advanced machine learning techniques to process your queries in real time.


How It Works:

-->Upload an Image: Start by uploading an image from your device. You can choose any image that you want to explore or inquire about.

-->Ask a Question: Once the image is uploaded, type in your question related to the content of the image. For example, "What is the object in the center?" or "Describe the background."

-->Get an Answer: Our sophisticated VQA model processes the image and your question to generate a precise answer. The response is based on the extensive training of our model on the VQAv2 dataset, ensuring high accuracy and relevance.
